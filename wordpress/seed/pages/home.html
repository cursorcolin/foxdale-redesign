Welcome to Foxdale Village

A community built on friendship, dignity, and respect.

Founded in 1985 by members of the State College Friends Meeting, Foxdale is a vibrant community offering independent living, personal care, skilled nursing, and memory support.

Visit, meet our residents and staff, and experience the warmth so many have come to call home.

Cottages & Apartments

Come home to comfortable living.

Choose from two distinctive home styles — classic cottage homes in nine garden neighborhoods, or apartment homes in our welcoming three-story building. Every residence is connected to the Community Center by covered or enclosed walkways, so you stay part of community life year-round.

Floor plans range from studio cottages to spacious two-bedroom, two-bath homes with sunrooms.

A Continuum of Care

Giving you peace of mind.

Dependable, high-quality healthcare is one of the most important considerations when choosing a Life Plan Community. At Foxdale, an on-site Medical Home, therapy services, and our licensed care neighborhoods — Darlington House and Anthony House — are right on campus, steps from the Community Center.

Whatever the future brings, the right support is here, within a community you know and trust.
