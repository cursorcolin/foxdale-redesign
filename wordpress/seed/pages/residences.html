Choose from garden cottages and apartment homes, each designed for comfort and easy access to community life.

Cottage Homes

Set within nine garden neighborhoods, cottages offer open or separate kitchens, all-season sunrooms, and direct access to the outdoors. Covered walkways connect every neighborhood to the Community Center.

Apartment Homes

Seven apartment floor plans offer easy living in a welcoming three-story building connected to the Community Center. Many include patios, balconies, or courtyard views.

The Design Center

Choose finishes for your new home.

Every home at Foxdale Village is prepared with care, and each one offers its own level of customization. Some homes are nearly move-in ready; others invite you to personalize more fully — selecting paint colors, choosing flooring, updating cabinetry, or exploring appliance options.

Once you’ve reserved your new home, you’ll meet with a Residency Planning Counselor in our Design Center. It’s a thoughtful, guided process designed to make your new home feel distinctly and beautifully yours.
