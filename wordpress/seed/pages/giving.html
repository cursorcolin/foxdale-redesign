Where Your Gift Goes

Eight funds, one community.

Foxdale Village is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit; contributions are tax deductible to the fullest extent of the law.

Community Fund

Helps residents facing unexpected financial hardship meet their monthly fees.

Entrance Fee Assistance

Helps those who qualify to live at Foxdale but lack the resources to pay the full entrance fee.

Givers of Care

Provides grants and short-term, no-interest loans to staff facing unanticipated emergency needs.

Landscape & Garden

Funds landscape and gardening projects across the Foxdale campus.

Outreach Fund

Funds new, mission-aligned programs that benefit older adults in the local area.

Training & Tuition

Supports continuing education for Foxdale staff — degree programs, certificates, and training.

Undesignated Gifts

Lets the Board of Trustees apply your gift where the need is greatest.

Memorial Gifts

Honors or remembers the individuals who are special to you.

Ways to Give

Choose how you give.

Beyond one-time online gifts, there are many ways to support Foxdale:

  • Write a check payable to Foxdale Village (include the fund name)
  • Donate securities — stock, bonds, or mutual funds
  • Establish a charitable gift annuity or charitable trust
  • Name Foxdale as a beneficiary of an IRA, 403(b), or life insurance policy
  • Make a qualified charitable distribution from your IRA, if eligible
  • Remember Foxdale in your will — with a fixed amount, a percentage, or the remainder of your estate
  • Invite memorial gifts to Foxdale in an obituary or memorial notice

Consult a legal, tax, or financial advisor when considering a planned gift. For more information, contact the Development Office at (814) 272-2115 or development@foxdalevillage.org. Official registration and financial information may be obtained from the Pennsylvania Department of State at (800) 732-0999; registration does not imply endorsement.
