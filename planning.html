Contract Options

Independence, security, and peace of mind.

Foxdale Village offers residential living for adults 62 and over. Both contract options offer access to high-quality healthcare — even if assets are later exhausted due to non-preventable circumstances. Residents can often use existing long-term care insurance.

Lifecare Contract

Predictable, protected rates

Maintain your active lifestyle while planning confidently for the future. With Lifecare, long-term care is available whenever you need it — at predictable and reduced rates.

  • The first 90 days of temporary care per episode at no additional charge
  • A portion of entrance and monthly fees may be claimed as medical expenses on your tax return*
  • Designed to provide lasting security and peace of mind for you and your loved ones

Fee-for-Service Contract

Flexible, pay as you go

A flexible option that lowers entrance and monthly fees while you live independently.

  • Lower entrance and monthly fees during independent living
  • Pay the full published daily rate only when healthcare services are needed
  • Works for short stays or longer-term transitions

*Consult your tax or financial advisor to determine your eligibility based on your individual situation.

How It Works

Four simple steps to Foxdale.

Join the Priority List

Complete a Priority List Agreement with a $1,000 deposit and $200 administrative fee. You choose when to start receiving offers — immediately or years later — and enjoy invitations to special events and dining experiences at Foxdale in the meantime.

Move to the Ready List

As your time frame approaches, we’ll add you to the Ready List and notify you when your preferred residence is available.

Complete Admissions

A Residency Planning Counselor guides you through the process, including financial and medical evaluations.

Design Your Home

Meet in our Design Center to select finishes and personalize your new cottage or apartment before move-in.

Premium Amenities

Everything included, for exceptional living.

Available to every resident, no matter which contract option best fits your needs.

Residential Living

  • Maintenance-free living with fewer responsibilities
  • Electric appliances: range, refrigerator/freezer, dishwasher, washer & dryer
  • Covered walkways from cottages to the Community Center
  • Enclosed walkway from apartments to the Community Center

In Your Monthly Fee

  • Housekeeping services
  • HD television and high-speed campus-wide Wi-Fi
  • Electricity included in apartments
  • Landline phone with free long distance
  • Interior and exterior maintenance, including appliances
  • Grounds care, snow removal, and lawn maintenance
  • 24-hour on-site staff

Transportation

  • Scheduled shopping trips
  • Free CATA bus service for those 65 and older
  • Free medical appointment transportation (within 15-mile radius)

Healthcare Services

  • Continuum of care across all stages of health
  • On-site Medical Home for those who choose it
  • Physical, occupational, and speech therapy
  • Pharmacy delivery and insurance paperwork assistance

Wellness Program

  • Fitness room with equipment and TVs
  • Free exercise classes led by Wellness staff
  • Therapy pool with group classes and open swim
  • Health, wellness, and nutrition programs
  • Opportunities for spiritual growth

For a Reasonable Fee

  • Electric vehicle (EV) charging
  • Transportation to select off-site events
  • Reserved covered parking and storage (when available)
  • Guest rooms for visitors as available
  • Beauty and barber services
