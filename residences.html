Planned with purpose. Built with care.

Two distinctive home styles — classic cottages and apartment homes — each designed for comfort, convenience, and a true sense of belonging.

Cottage Homes

Nestled in nine naturally beautiful neighborhoods, our cottages offer spacious living with open or separate kitchens, all-season sunrooms, and direct access to the outdoors. Every cottage neighborhood is connected to the Community Center by a covered walkway — so you stay engaged in community life year round.

Apartment Homes

Our apartment homes fill a welcoming three-story building with an enclosed walkway to the Community Center. Seven unique floor plans — many with patios, balconies, or courtyard views — offer the ease of apartment living within a close-knit neighborhood.

Residences range from 635 to 1,450 square feet, including three studio cottages — check with your Residency Planning Counselor for availability. Every home includes a modern kitchen with locally crafted cabinetry, in-home laundry, and a 24-hour emergency call system. Dimensions and configurations may vary by residence; half-bath options carry an additional charge.

The Design Center

Designing the details.

Every home at Foxdale Village is prepared with care, and each one offers its own level of customization. Some homes are nearly move-in ready; others invite you to personalize more fully — selecting paint colors, choosing flooring, updating cabinetry, or exploring appliance options.

Once you’ve reserved your new home, you’ll meet with a Residency Planning Counselor in our Design Center. It’s a thoughtful, guided process designed to make your new home feel distinctly and beautifully yours.
